# ARANOVA: Brand Asset Guidelines
*The Official Playbook for Nigeria's Most Iconic Event Experience*

---

## 1. Brand Core & Identity
ARANOVA is not a prop rental company; it is an **experience engineering firm**.
- **Mission**: To construct unforgettable, viral celebrity moments for Africa's elite.
- **Tone of Voice**: Assertive, Elegant, Confident, and Curiosity-Driven. Avoid cheap, desperate sales jargon. Never say "rent our booth." Say "Secure your date," "Elevate your reception," or "Give your guests the luxury treatment."

---

## 2. Typography Guidelines
Consistency in typography is what separates premium brands from amateurs. 
Always stick to this hierarchy across all digital and print assets:

### Primary Typeface (Headings & Titles)
**Cormorant Garamond** (or similar elegant serif like Playfair Display).
- *Usage*: Main headlines, magazine cover titles, hero section hooks.
- *Styling*: Frequently stylized in *italic* to emphasize luxury and romance.

### Secondary Typeface (Body & UI)
**DM Sans** or **Montserrat**.
- *Usage*: Paragraphs, buttons, pricing tables, UI elements.
- *Styling*: Keep it extremely clean, legible, and slightly tracked out (letter-spacing: 1px) for a breathable, modern look.

---

## 3. Official Color Palette
Aranova is defined by its stark, high-contrast, moody aesthetic.

- **Deep Obsidian (Primary Background)**
  - Hex: `#0A0A0A`
  - Used for 90% of website backgrounds to create that "dark mode luxury" feel.
- **Aranova Gold (Primary Accent)**
  - Hex: `#C9A84C` or `#D4AF37`
  - Used sparingly for buttons, active calendar weekends, and key highlights. Never overuse gold, or it becomes tacky.
- **Pure White / Pearlescent (Text & Contrast)**
  - Hex: `#FFFFFF` / `rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` 
  - Used for sharp, readable body text.

---

## 4. Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo:** Serifed "ARANOVA" spelled in full caps.
- **Clear Space:** Always allow at least 2 inches (or 50px) of breathing room around the logo. Never crowd it with other graphics or text.
- **Color Restrictions:** The logo should only ever appear in Aranova Gold `#C9A84C` or Pure White `#FFFFFF`. Never place the logo on a busy, high-contrast background that makes it unreadable.

---

## 5. Visual & Photography Style
The Aranova Box is a highly visual product. The way it is photographed dictates the perceived value.
- **Lighting**: Dark, cinematic, moody. Rely on the bright LED studio lights inside the box rather than flattening the image with a camera flash.
- **Focus**: Capture the *emotion* of the guests inside the box, not just the physical hardware. We sell the feeling of being a star.
- **Marketplace Aesthetics**: For the Marketplace suite, use hyper-clean, well-lit, minimalist backgrounds to show the premium nature of the sourced home goods. No messy backgrounds. No cluttered rooms.
