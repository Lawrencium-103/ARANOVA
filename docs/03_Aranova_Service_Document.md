# ARANOVA Service & Operations Document

This document outlines the standard operating procedures (SOPs) and service definitions for both arms of the Aranova ecosystem.

---

## PART A: The ARANOVA Box Experience

### 1. Booking & Pre-Event Flow
- **Inquiry:** Client submits the "Official Booking Order" via the website. Form captures: Date, State, Venue, Theme, and Package.
- **Confirmation:** Automated WhatsApp invoice is generated. 25% non-refundable deposit is required to lock the date.
- **Design Phase:** The Lead Designer requests the couple's names, event hashtag, and specific cover lines. A digital mockup of the luxury magazine panel is sent within 48 hours for client approval.
- **Final Clearance:** The remaining balance is cleared strictly 48 hours prior to the event.

### 2. Event Day Execution (The 90/15 Rule)
- **Arrival:** The Aranova logistics team and assigned Brand Ambassador(s) arrive strictly **90 minutes** before the event start time.
- **Setup:** The modular box is assembled and premium LED studio lighting is calibrated within **15 minutes**.
- **Operation:** Brand Ambassadors actively direct guests into the box, suggest poses, verify lighting angles, and ensure seamless traffic flow. 
- **Teardown:** Executed discreetly at the end of the contracted runtime (4 to 6 hours) without disrupting the ongoing event or the host's experience.

## PART B: The ARANOVA Marketplace (Sourcing Suite)

### 1. Vendor Onboarding & Quality Control
The Marketplace is an exclusive, strictly curated ecosystem. Not every vendor is accepted.
- **Application:** Vendors apply by submitting their professional portfolio, pricing floor, and three verifiable past-client references.
- **Vetting:** The Marketplace Manager reviews the application against Aranova's strict luxury standards. 
- **Approval:** Approved vendors receive verified badges and are listed in specific high-end categories (Photography, Mixology, Elite Decor, etc.).

### 2. Client Sourcing Flow
- **Discovery:** Couples use the Marketplace's aesthetic, filtered grid to find vendors matching their specific vibe and budget requirements.
- **Inquiry:** Direct, transparent communication is initiated via the platform.
- **Booking & Escrow:** To guarantee absolute trust, Aranova offers a secure milestone-based payment hold. Funds are released to the vendor only upon successful completion of contracted milestones, protecting the couple from no-shows and vendors from non-payment.

### 3. Monetization & Revenue Generation
- **Premium Listing Fees:** Monthly or annual subscription for vendors to maintain premium marketplace positioning and analytics.
- **Transaction Commission:** A flat percentage fee on bookings facilitated through the Aranova trusted escrow system.
