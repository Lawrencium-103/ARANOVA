## 🚀 The 10-Post Viral Strategy (Curiosity & Values)

**Content Strategy Rules:** Follow the strict 3-hashtag rule for maximum TikTok/IG Reels virality. Use high-quality visuals. Never show the *entire* box in the first 3 seconds of a video—build curiosity first.

### Post 1: The Teaser (Curiosity Focus)
**Visual:** Video of a guest stepping inside a dark, luxurious box, then a massive camera flash illuminates the frame. Fast cut to their reaction.
**Copy:** We brought a secret box to a Lagos wedding… and the guests refused to leave it. Any guesses what happens inside? 👀✨
