# About ARANOVA
*Nigeria’s Most Iconic Event Experience*

## Our Story
ARANOVA was born out of a simple observation in the Nigerian event industry: hosts spend millions on decor, yet guest experiences remain stagnant. The standard 360-degree camera has become expected and uninspiring. We envisioned an experience that didn't just capture a moment, but elevated the guest's perceived status. The result? The ARANOVA Box—a life-sized, fully customizable luxury magazine cover that transforms every guest into a celebrity.

## Our Mission
To engineer unforgettable, viral moments for high-end events by merging luxury physical installations with instant social media shareability.

## Our Vision
To become the premier luxury event experience and vendor sourcing platform across Africa, synonymous with exclusivity, flawless execution, and high-society celebrations.

1. **Uncompromised Luxury:** From the materials of our box to our team's uniform, excellence is our baseline.
2. **Built for Virality:** We don't just build props; we build organic marketing engines that amplify our clients' personal brands.
3. **Flawless Execution:** Arrival 90 minutes early. Setup in 15 minutes. Zero stress for the host. 
4. **Discretion & Trust:** We handle the most important days of our clients' lives. Total privacy and data security are guaranteed.
